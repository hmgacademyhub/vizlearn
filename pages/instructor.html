A free classroom planning layer for using VizLearn in a school, bootcamp, YouTube tutorial, virtual class, or self-paced cohort. It generates lesson flow, activity timing, assessment prompts, and grading rubrics without AI APIs.

Why Instructor Mode exists

Pedagogy

VizLearn should support not only individual learners but also teachers. This page turns the platform into a reusable teaching toolkit: objective, demonstration, guided practice, independent task, assessment, and reflection.

No AI API: Plans are generated from transparent templates and timing rules. You can edit the text freely.
